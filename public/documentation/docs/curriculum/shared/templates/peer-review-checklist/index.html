Peer Review Checklist

Purpose: This checklist provides structured guidance for reviewing another person's assurance case.

How to use: Work through each section, providing constructive feedback. Focus on being helpful rather than critical.

Review Process​

Before You Begin​

  • [ ] Read the entire assurance case before providing detailed feedback
  • [ ] Understand the context and goals
  • [ ] Approach the review with constructive intent
  • [ ] Note both strengths and areas for improvement

Review Mindset​

Good peer review is:

  • ✅ Constructive and helpful
  • ✅ Specific and actionable
  • ✅ Balanced (strengths and improvements)
  • ✅ Respectful and collaborative

Avoid:

  • ❌ Vague criticism ("this doesn't work")
  • ❌ Prescriptive demands ("you must do X")
  • ❌ Personal judgments
  • ❌ Focusing only on weaknesses

For the Author: Receiving Feedback​

When You Receive This Review​

Remember:

  • Peer review is a gift of time and attention
  • All feedback is offered to help improve your work
  • You don't have to implement every suggestion
  • Consider the feedback carefully, then make informed decisions
  • It's okay to disagree, but understand why you disagree
  • Ask for clarification if feedback is unclear

Next Steps:

  1. Read the entire review before responding
  2. Take time to reflect (sleep on it if needed)
  3. Identify which feedback resonates most
  4. Create an action plan for revisions
  5. Thank your reviewer
  6. If helpful, discuss major points with the reviewer
