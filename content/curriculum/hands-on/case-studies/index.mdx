# Case Studies

Explore real-world examples of assurance cases across different domains. These case studies demonstrate how the TEA methodology can be applied to various AI and data-driven systems.

### Environmental

### Education

- **[Student Learning Assessment](./student-learning-assessment)** - AI systems for educational assessment and feedback

## How to Use These Case Studies

Each case study includes:

1. **Context** - Background on the domain and system
2. **Assurance Case Structure** - The complete argument structure
3. **Evidence Examples** - Types of evidence used
4. **Discussion Questions** - Points for reflection and learning

These case studies can be used for:

- **Self-study** - Work through examples at your own pace
- **Workshops** - Group activities and discussions
- **Templates** - Starting points for your own cases
