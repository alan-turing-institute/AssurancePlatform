## Most Common Terms

Here are the most frequently used terms you'll encounter when working with the TEA Platform:

### Core Concepts

* **[Assurance](../shared/glossary#assurance)** - Process of building justified trust through structured argumentation
* **[Assurance Case](../shared/glossary#assurance-case)** - Structured argument demonstrating a system meets its goals
* **[Argument](../shared/glossary#argument)** - Logical structure linking claims to evidence
* **[Trustworthy and Ethical Assurance (TEA)](../shared/glossary#trustworthy-and-ethical-assurance-tea)** - The methodology and platform for argument-based assurance

### Element Types

* **[Goal Claim](../shared/glossary#goal-claim)** - Top-level normative claim directing the assurance case
* **[Property Claim](../shared/glossary#property-claim)** - Specific claim about a system property
* **[Strategy](../shared/glossary#strategy)** - Explanation of how goals/claims are decomposed
* **[Evidence](../shared/glossary#evidence)** - Information supporting claims
* **[Context](../shared/glossary#context)** - Scope, boundaries, and constraints

### Evidence & Quality

* **[Evidence Quality](../shared/glossary#evidence-quality)** - Assessment of evidence using three criteria: relevance, reliability, sufficiency
* **[Relevance](../shared/glossary#relevance)** - How directly evidence supports a claim
* **[Reliability](../shared/glossary#reliability)** - How trustworthy the evidence source is
* **[Sufficiency](../shared/glossary#sufficiency)** - Whether there's enough evidence to support the claim

### Methodology

* **[Deliberation](../shared/glossary#deliberation)** - Careful consideration of different perspectives
* **[Critical Reflection](../shared/glossary#critical-reflection)** - Systematic examination of assumptions and reasoning
* **[Justified Trust](../shared/glossary#justified-trust)** - Trust based on evidence and reasoning
* **[Stakeholder](../shared/glossary#stakeholder)** - Anyone with interest in or affected by the system
