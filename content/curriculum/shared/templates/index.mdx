# Templates

This section provides reusable templates to support your work with assurance cases and the TEA curriculum.

## Available Templates

### For Learners

| Template | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| [Reflection Template](./reflection-template) | Structured prompts for reflecting on your learning and assurance case work |
| [Peer Review Checklist](./peer-review-checklist) | Guidelines for reviewing others' assurance cases |
| [Evidence Quality Rubric](./evidence-quality-rubric) | Criteria for evaluating the quality of evidence in assurance cases |
| [Team Collaboration Agreement](./team-collaboration-agreement) | Framework for establishing team norms when working on assurance cases together |

### For Curriculum Developers

| Template | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| [Module Template](./module-template) | Standardised structure for creating new curriculum modules |

## How to Use These Templates

1. **Browse** the templates above to find one that suits your needs
2. **Copy** the template content to your own document
3. **Customise** by filling in the sections relevant to your work
4. **Share** your completed work with peers or instructors for feedback

## Contributing New Templates

If you have a template that would benefit the TEA community, please consider contributing it:

1. Follow the structure of existing templates
2. Include clear instructions and examples
3. Submit via [GitHub Pull Request](https://github.com/alan-turing-institute/AssurancePlatform/pulls)
